SECURITY & MANAGEMENT


Building Site CCTV provides real-time access to CCTV systems that allow clients and site managers to view your site remotely from any device with an internet connection.

  • Building Site CCTV can be set up as a permanent or temporary solution
  • Our systems are solar powered and can be deployed in any location
  • High resolution cameras with night vision capabilities
  • 24/7 monitoring and recording
  • Mobile app access for live viewing and playback

The Building Site CCTV system is designed to be simple, reliable and transportable.

ABOUT OUR SITE CCTV


The Building Site CCTV system is a high resolution solar powered camera system designed to provide builders with the ability to monitor their sites 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

This system was designed and developed by Building Site CCTV owner Jim Gallagher who has been installing security CCTV & Access Control Systems for a number of Melbourne based builders and trade professionals.
