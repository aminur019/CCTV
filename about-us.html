PRODUCT INFORMATION


Building Site CCTV established in 2020 is an Australian Based company manufacturing Solar CCTV Systems. Our Building Site CCTV system offers a high resolution solar powered camera system. Designed to provide Builders the ability to view their site 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

The system was designed and developed by Building Site CCTV's owner Jarryd Miller who has been in the security industry for over 15 years. Utilising this experience has enabled us to build and develop our Builders Eye Range of systems which has resulted in Building Site CCTV quickly growing to become one of Victoria's biggest Solar CCTV manufacturers & providers.

We now provide Solar CCTV solutions to some of Victoria's largest construction companies, local governments and State government departments.

Building Site CCTV is one of the only companies that provide the option to hire and purchase our systems. Our official sales channel BSCCTVSHOP.AU sells all of our systems Australia Wide which has seen systems deployed to Queensland, Tasmania, New South Wales, South Australian and Western Australia.
